How To Use

Pick a word, any word, and let EtymoloGenius plot its path from various origin languages and countries to modern day English, or click on a country to see an example word. All queries are made to the Oxford English Dictionary API. (NOTE: Plotted paths are approximate and are mostly (but not always) in the correct order. This algorithm is consistently updated. Not all countries contain recorded words connected to English.)

Interesting words to try: barbecue, biology, mohawk.
